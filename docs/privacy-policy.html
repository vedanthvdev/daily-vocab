Dayink Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Dayink (“the app”) is an offline vocabulary learning utility for iOS and Android.

What we collect

We do not collect personal data.

Dayink does not require an account and does not include analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. Nothing is uploaded to our servers because the app has no network features for user content.

What stays on your device

The app may store the following only on your device:

  • Your preferred difficulty level (Beginner, Intermediate, or Hard)
  • Today’s locked word and short explanation
  • A local history of words shown this year (for the in-app History screen)
  • The same word snapshot shared with the Lock Screen / home screen widget via on-device App Group / shared preferences

You can remove this data by deleting the app.

Permissions

Dayink may play a short in-app sound. It does not use the microphone, location, contacts, photos, or tracking across apps.

Children’s privacy

Dayink is not directed at children under 13 and is not marketed as a children’s app.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
